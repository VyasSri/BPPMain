Worst fit

Description

The Worst Fit (WF) algorithm works by placing each item into the bin where it will leave the most remaining space, attempting to keep the bins as balanced as possible. If no bin can accommodate the item, a new bin is opened. While Worst Fit shares a similar time complexity with First Fit and Best Fit, it typically underperforms in practice, often leading to underutilized bins.
